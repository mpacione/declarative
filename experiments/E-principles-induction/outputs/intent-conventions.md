# Intent conventions

- Active states and primary selections (like chosen tools, active slider tracks, or selected list items) consistently use a high-contrast neon chartreuse fill with black text or icons.
- Floating toolbars and contextual menus utilize stark white, heavily rounded pill shapes with soft drop shadows to maintain visual separation from the canvas.
- Dismissive or secondary actions (like 'Cancel') use solid black pill-shaped buttons with white text, contrasting sharply with the bright primary action buttons.
- Canvas object selection is denoted by a thin, bright blue bounding box with square corner handles, ensuring high visibility against varied user imagery.
