## Designer-authored sections (auto-induced v1)

### Voice
Bold, utilitarian, and highly energetic. Stark black-and-white structural elements combined with punchy neon chartreuse accents evoke a modern, digital-native tool designed for quick creation. The UI typography remains strictly neutral and minimal, deliberately stepping back to let the chaotic, highly saturated user-generated meme content take center stage.

### Intent conventions
- Active states and primary selections (like chosen tools, active slider tracks, or selected list items) consistently use a high-contrast neon chartreuse fill with black text or icons.
- Floating toolbars and contextual menus utilize stark white, heavily rounded pill shapes with soft drop shadows to maintain visual separation from the canvas.
- Dismissive or secondary actions (like 'Cancel') use solid black pill-shaped buttons with white text, contrasting sharply with the bright primary action buttons.
- Canvas object selection is denoted by a thin, bright blue bounding box with square corner handles, ensuring high visibility against varied user imagery.

### Exclusions
- No dark mode variants are observed; the system relies entirely on stark white surfaces for its panels and toolbars.
- No serif or decorative typography is used in the UI, strictly adhering to a neutral sans-serif to avoid clashing with user content.
- No sharp, 90-degree corners on UI components; everything from buttons to floating panels uses generous, pill-like border radii.

### Style lineage
- Reminiscent of modern mobile creative tools like Instagram Stories or Canva (floating pill menus, bottom sheets, soft shadows) crossed with the high-contrast, neon-and-black utilitarian aesthetic often seen in Web3 and digital-native youth brands.
